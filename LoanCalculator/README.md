## Overview

A simple **HTML/JavaScript-based loan calculator** that computes the Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) for different loan types :- Home, Car, and Personal.

## Rules

| Loan Type | Minimum Amount | Maximum Duration | Interest Rate (%) |
| --------- | -------------- | ---------------- | ----------------- |
| Home      | ₹5,00,000      | 30 years         | 9%                |
| Car       | ₹1,00,000      | 7 years          | 12%               |
| Personal  | ₹10,000        | 5 years          | 15%               |
